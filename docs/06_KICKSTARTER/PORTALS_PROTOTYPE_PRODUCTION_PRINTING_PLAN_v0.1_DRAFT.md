## 1. Founder question

```text
Plan and cost: how to make the Portals prototype and where to print it?
```

## 2. Recommended production strategy

Do not start with mass production.

Recommended route:

```text
Phase 1: cheap paper prototype.
Phase 2: nice prototype for playtests.
Phase 3: review/Kickstarter prototype.
Phase 4: quote mass production after campaign validation.
```

Reason: the rules, component count, card counts, board size, box size, token counts, and art are still changing.

## 3. Phase 1: home / local print prototype

Goal: test gameplay, not beauty.

Components:

```text
- 1 paper board printed on A3/A2 sheets.
- 80-120 simple cards printed on paper and sleeved with backing cards.
- generic meeples/pawns.
- ordinary dice plus temporary custom risk die stickers.
- acrylic gems or cheap colored stones as Sparks.
- paper portal markers.
```

Estimated cost in Ireland/UK/EU:

```text
EUR 30-100 for one crude playable copy.
```

Use for:

```text
- first 10-20 playtests;
- fixing rules;
- cutting boring systems;
- proving turn loop.
```

## 4. Phase 2: polished small prototype

Goal: make 3-5 copies for family/friends/local playtests.

Components:

```text
- mounted or foldable board.
- 100-150 proper cards.
- printed player boards.
- printed rulebook.
- better Spark tokens.
- basic box.
- simple pawns and dice.
```

Recommended services:

```text
- BoardGamesMaker / MakePlayingCards for one-off and small runs.
- The Game Crafter for print-on-demand board game prototypes.
- local Irish/UK print shop for A2/A1 board + cards if cheaper.
```

Estimated cost:

```text
EUR 100-250 per good prototype copy.
EUR 300-800 for 3-5 copies.
```

## 5. Phase 3: Kickstarter review prototype

Goal: make 5-10 impressive copies for video, photos, reviewers, and demo tables.

Components:

```text
- high-quality 3-ring board.
- 120-180 cards.
- 6 character boards.
- 60-100 Spark tokens.
- 30 portal markers.
- 2 dice.
- box mockup.
- rulebook.
- player aids.
```

Estimated cost:

```text
EUR 800-2500 for 5-10 review/demo prototypes.
```

This is the version for Kickstarter photos and previewers.

## 6. Phase 4: manufacturing quote for campaign

Do not print 500+ before campaign proof.

For manufacturing quotes, request quotes from:

```text
- Panda Game Manufacturing.
- LongPack Games.
- PrintNinja.
- BoardGamesMaker / QP Group.
- Whatz Games.
- Gameland.
```

Typical serious manufacturer requirements:

```text
- full component list;
- final size of board;
- final card count;
- final box size;
- punchboard/token specs;
- dice specs;
- rulebook page count;
- estimated order quantity.
```

## 7. Recommended first box spec for quote

Use this as starting quote spec:

```text
Title: Portals
Box: medium square family board game box
Board: quad-fold board, about 560 x 560 mm or 600 x 600 mm
Cards: 120 poker-size cards, 63 x 88 mm
Character cards/boards: 6 tarot-size or mini boards
Spark tokens: 72 acrylic/plastic gems or punchboard tokens
Portal markers: 30 punchboard tokens
Player pawns: 6 plastic/wood pawns
Dice: 1 movement die + 1 custom risk die
Rulebook: 16 pages
Player aids: 6 cards
Insert: simple cardboard insert or no insert for first run
```

## 8. Target unit economics for Kickstarter

For a EUR/USD 39-69 pledge, the landed cost must be controlled.

Approximate targets:

```text
Manufacturing cost target: 20-30% of pledge.
Shipping charged separately if possible.
Platform/payment fees: roughly 8-10%.
Marketing/video/prototypes must be budgeted separately.
Contingency: at least 10-15%.
```

Avoid heavy miniatures in first edition.
