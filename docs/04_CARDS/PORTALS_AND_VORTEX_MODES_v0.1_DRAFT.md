## Design decision

A Portal in IVDIVO is not only teleportation. It is a controlled or unstable transition mechanism between:

- worlds;
- zones;
- orbitals;
- laws;
- Ecopolis buildings;
- roots;
- forms;
- states of the character;
- levels of action.

Each Portal card includes:

- source;
- destination;
- opening condition;
- key or permission;
- cost;
- risk;
- throughput;
- direction of influence;
- transfer consequences;
- control / damage / contamination / blocking logic;
- stabilization method;
- closing method;
- playtest note.

## Playtest rule

Portals must never become free universal movement. Every portal must preserve at least one of the following constraints:

- cost;
- permission;
- throughput limit;
- risk;
- contamination possibility;
- damage possibility;
- stabilization requirement;
- closing requirement.

Vortex Modes are not separate locations. They are temporary states of portal behavior under pressure, rupture, fire, orbital mismatch, or synthesis action.
