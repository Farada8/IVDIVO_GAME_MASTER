## 6. Status taxonomy

Use these statuses:

```text
GAME-DRAFT — already adapted to playable card mechanics.
SOURCE-CANDIDATE — useful source-law idea, not yet balanced.
FOUNDER-CONFIRMED — directly confirmed by founder.
HIGH-MODE / FA — only for experienced/high/campaign mode.
TO-SIMPLIFY — too numerically or metaphysically dense for mass play.
DUPLICATE / MERGE — overlaps another law and should be merged.
REJECTED — founder rejected this direction.
SUPERSEDED — replaced by newer terminology or mechanics.
```

## 7. Design translation rule

Many source-law phrases use huge numbers or metaphysical scale. For a playable mass game, translate them into mechanical thresholds.

Examples:

```text
16384 realities -> threshold gate / high portal density / many-presence mechanic.
32768 deeds -> campaign-scale collective-mission requirement.
8 evolutions -> high-mode victory requirement: express 8 evolution lines simultaneously.
256/512/1024 structures -> level tiers, not literal arithmetic burden during play.
Absolute Fire -> temporary high fire state, not permanent invincibility.
All resources become one -> short Synthesis-mode conversion, not always-on free economy.
```
