## Purpose

This folder is the dedicated GitHub workspace for **Game 01: Portals of Stars**.

The game is currently treated as the first small-family-game product candidate inside the wider IVDIVO tabletop/IP universe. It must remain light, visual, family-friendly, fast to prototype, and commercially testable.

## Current positioning

**Portals of Stars** is not a heavy metaphysical system for the first product release. It is a small family adventure game about portals, Sparks, crystals, heroes, treasures, risk, missions, and a living board.

The deep IVDIVO universe may remain as lore and future expansion material, but the first public entry must be instantly understandable:

> Bright family fantasy adventure through portals, Sparks, treasures, heroes, and living worlds.

## Core product principle

Do not make a beautiful roll-and-move board.

Make a small living world where every turn offers at least one real choice:

- where to move;
- what to collect;
- whether to risk;
- which portal to open;
- which card to play;
- whether to chase a mission, treasure, hero, or gate;
- how to reach the Great Portal before the Catastrophe.

## Active design pillars

1. **Readable board** — understandable in 2 minutes.
2. **Decision every turn** — no empty movement turns.
3. **Visible rewards** — Sparks, crystals, keys, treasures, artifacts.
4. **Portals with cost and risk** — not free teleportation.
5. **Family-safe tension** — danger without cruel punishment.
6. **No evil PvP** — interaction, trading, racing, shared danger, but no bullying the losing player.
7. **Playtest fast** — small rules, paper prototype, cheap components.
8. **Product thinking** — table presence, Kickstarter clarity, retail potential.

## Working component direction v0.1

- 1 board with four elemental / star-portaled zones.
- Central Great Portal.
- Sparks: 4 types + Star Spark joker.
- Crystals and Portal Keys.
- Player boards with character, hearts/energy, resource slots, hero slots, artifact slots.
- 5 key decks: Portals, Heroes, Missions, Treasures/Artifacts, Catastrophes.
- Optional bag system for push-your-luck exploration.
- Movement die or simple movement cards.
- No large personal deckbuilding in v0.1.
