## Approved decisions

### CANON / FOUNDER-DECISION

1. GitHub is the primary textual source of truth for project knowledge, decisions, research, mechanics, worlds, cards, campaigns, and AI results.
2. Google Drive remains the storage and collaboration layer for Google Docs, Word/PDF, images, presentations, layouts, prototypes, and large or binary files.
3. NotebookLM is an analysis and retrieval layer, not the authoritative storage layer.
4. A migration is not considered complete when only a summary or truncated connector extract is copied; the complete source file must be preserved or converted without loss.
5. The dream/fate/reverse-time concept belongs to future card-game projects, not to the current base-game architecture.

## New ideas

### IDEAS — future card-game project

Working title: Dream / Fate / Reverse Time.

Core premise:

- actions happen first in dreams;
- later they manifest in waking life as what people call fate;
- dream layers differ in depth and causal power;
- at deeper levels, time flows backward from consequence to cause.

Potential causal sequence:

`DREAM → EVENT PREFIGURATION → DREAM CHOICE → FATE SHIFT → WAKING MANIFESTATION`

Reverse-time sequence:

`CONSEQUENCE → TRACE → SYMBOL → CAUSE → CHOICE NODE`

Possible dream levels:

1. surface dream;
2. symbolic dream;
3. fate-bearing dream;
4. reverse dream;
5. deep fate dream.

This remains a future standalone card-game or expansion concept and is not current canon.
